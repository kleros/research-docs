# Case 35 — Kleros Juror Assessment

**Court:** Kleros "Blockchain No Técnica"
**Promo / product at issue:** FIAT transfer via Lemon (CVU/alias) — user-initiated transfer to a wrong alias
**Amount in dispute:** ARS 3,940.00
**Question:** Must Lemon refund the user for an inter-bank transfer that the user himself executed to an incorrect alias?

## Options
- Favor User
- Favor Lemon
- Refuse to Arbitrate

## Governing rules
1. Política Section A — Protección, Información, Transparencia, Buena fe, In dubio (tiebreaker), Educación, No discriminación.
2. Section B — T&Cs operate only insofar as they are compatible with Section A.
3. Digifin/Lemon FIAT T&Cs — user instructs the transfer; once executed via the national payments rail (CVU/CBU), the transfer is irreversible and the destination institution holds the funds.
4. Argentine inter-bank payment rules (BCRA): transferencias inmediatas are final and irrevocable once accredited at destination; reversal requires voluntary return by the receiving party.

## Burden of proof
The user bears the burden of showing that Lemon — not the user, not the destination bank, not the unrelated recipient — caused or is legally responsible for the loss. On undisputed facts the user must show a duty Lemon breached.

- Decorative logos.

## Application
- **Protección:** Lemon's protective duty does not extend to undoing a user's own keystroke error on an irreversible rail when no Lemon system failure is alleged.
- **Información:** No allegation that Lemon misinformed the user about the recipient. The receipt clearly displays recipient name, CUIT, CBU and destination bank before/at completion; the user did not catch the mismatch.
- **Transparencia:** The product (instant transfer to alias entered by the user) operated AS PRESENTED. No hidden term is invoked against the user.
- **Buena fe:** No bad-faith conduct by Lemon is alleged or shown. Lemon executed the instruction the user gave.
- **In dubio:** Not triggered — facts are undisputed; the user admits the error. No tie to break.
- **Educación:** No educational-failure claim is made; the alias/CBU mechanism is standard and the receipt itself surfaces the destination data.
- **No discriminación:** Not in issue.
- **T&C compatibility:** The irreversibility clause is compatible with Section A — it reflects the underlying payment-system reality, not a self-serving carve-out. Section A does not require Lemon to manufacture a refund from its own funds for a user-caused misdirection to a third party.

## Jurisdiction / Morality / Validity
Within scope of the Blockchain No Técnica consumer-protection court. Claim is sincere; not frivolous; arbitrable.

## Schelling-Point check
A diligent juror would converge on Favor Lemon: the user admits the error, the receipt confirms the funds reached the alias he typed, and Lemon has no legal power to claw funds back from an unrelated account at Mercado Pago. Holding Lemon liable would convert it into an insurer of user typing errors — a result not required by Política Section A.

## Bias audit
Sympathy for a small-amount consumer loss is acknowledged and set aside. The decision turns on causation and the absence of any breach by Lemon, not on the user's identity or the amount.

## VOTE
**Favor Lemon**

**Confidence:** high

## On-chain justification (2–3 short paragraphs, English)

The user candidly admits he entered the wrong alias when instructing the transfer, and the receipt he himself submits confirms ARS 3,940.00 was successfully delivered to the alias as typed (Viviana Ayelen Ramirez, CBU 0000003100097822477480, Mercado Pago) on 15 June 2024. Lemon executed the user's instruction exactly as given. Under Argentine immediate-transfer rules — reflected in Digifin's T&Cs — once funds are accredited at the destination institution, the originating wallet has no unilateral power to reverse them; recovery depends on the voluntary cooperation of the unrelated recipient.

Applied to Política Section A: Protección, Información and Transparencia are unimpaired because the product operated as presented and the receipt surfaces the destination data; Buena fe is not breached because Lemon followed the user's express instruction; Educación and No discriminación are not in issue; In dubio is inapplicable because the operative facts are undisputed and admitted. The T&C term on irreversibility is compatible with Section A — it mirrors the underlying payments-rail reality rather than asserting a self-serving immunity.

Section A does not impose on Lemon the duty to indemnify a user for his own keystroke error directed at a third-party account beyond Lemon's control. The claim therefore fails on causation and on the absence of any breach by Lemon. Vote: Favor Lemon.
